PIN CUT!

PIN CUT is an application that will display all salons and barbers close by in Beirut district.

It will allow customers to search by desired location, service, quality and price ranges.

Description!

The application won't focus on a certain age or gender however an opposition to that.

It will focus on all people of various ages, and genders since all individuals are included in
personal hygiene and self-care.

Benefit!

For salons and barbers, the benefit is simply advertising for their service excellence and promotions
with a monthly fee that won't be generally high.
